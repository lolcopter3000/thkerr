Composition


			
Music Piece

			 
@ Edmund Burke Theatre 
 
			Composed and directed by me, 2025 
 
			Quietude and epiphany inspired by Neurodivergence and Quaker Meetings. 

			It was non-verbal and made with a neurodivergent focus. One of 3 shows chosen to headline DU Players' 35-show Summerfest.

			I played drums, guitars, basses, keys, vocals. Compared favourably to Brian Eno! And Alex G! Thanks everyone.
