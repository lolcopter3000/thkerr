Prize

This is a really fantastic album to me. I admire how it sits at a pretty stable dynamic thoughout. I admire also the quality of the writing; it is simple and beautiful and always interesting rhythmically and melodically. It is not pretentious at all. It is never insubstantial. It suffers from not declaring itself enough - live, they don't seem to be having too much fun, or don't appear confident. She undersells herself this way. Though why would you want to sell yourself anyway?
