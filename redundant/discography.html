Thomas Kerr - Music Piece
Acousmatic music made for performance in the Edmund Burke Theatre at Trinity College. I directed, with Laura Pendlebury as beloved confidante... she also directed Arsh Vohra, the actor. It is not complete without the visual but the music is a record of the performance. Fiona Murray managed it and Jack Leitch did video design.
Peter McMahon plays fiddle on one song; Aodhán Collins harmonica on Twice Shy.

Thomas Kerr - Music from fish.
Made for Laura Pendlebury's show, performed 02/28/26 at Smock Alley. The show's text was pre-recorded by voice actors and played during performance, accompained by this music. I play guitar, bass, synths, harmonium, piano, a few other things.
John Delhomme played French Horn and Sofia Féria played cello.

Thomas Kerr Trio - Strings Forever
Piece devised and performed with Sam Armstrong and Sofia Féria 10/04/26.
